Contour

Since most people use their thumbs and fingers to interact with their phones, the relatively large surface area of a thumb compared to small buttons can make it difficult to accurately. This often forces users to be overly precise when pressing buttons. To solve this, the feature increases the effective buton size by adding an invisible contour around each button, without changing the overall Ui layout.
